Teleglitch

Teleglitch is brutal; defenestration-of-monitors kind of brutal. You will die, a lot. But if you succeed, it will represent a true achievement.

Helldivers

Helldivers is a solid cooperative multiplayer game. The best way I can describe it is as the unholy offspring of Magicka and Alien Swarm. If you haven't played either or both of these, no worries.

Crawl

Crawl is a piece of local multiplayer genius. You and up to three friends take turns controlling a hero. As the hero, you want to level up and escape the dungeon. Everyone else is pitted against the hero, as the various hazards of the dungeon (including the bosses!).

Infinifactory

Infinifactory is addicting. Really, really addicting.

Hyper Light Drifter

Hyper Light Drifter is a frantic, graceful and sleek high action role-playing game. TL;DR: Fucking get it. If you want to know more, read on.

Samurai Gunn

Samurai Gunn was released in December of 2013. Developer Teknopants conceived the idea for a fun party game (at a party, natch.), and executed it flawlessly.
